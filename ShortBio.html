Eli Kaufman Geography (GIS) Student

I am currently a junior majoring in geography on the geographic information science (BS) track. As of this point in my college education, some of my academic accomplishments include developing a strong foundation in skills related to both the use of technologies and spatial applications related to GIS, as well as the ability to interpret results to identify correlation and spatial trends in data. My GIS-focused course work has included Foundations of GIS, LiDAR: Principles/Application, Intro to Geographic Databases, and currently, Geographic Visualization, all of which have helped me to develop and reinforce my skills in map design, database management, and geoprocessing.
